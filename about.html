Sharing the World Through Images

Our mission is to make visual content accessible to everyone

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ImageDonate began as a small community of photographers who believed in the power of sharing. What started as a passion project among friends has grown into a global platform with thousands of contributors and millions of images donated.

We recognized that while the internet is full of images, finding truly free, high-quality visuals was challenging for many creators, educators, and non-profits. Our platform bridges this gap by connecting generous photographers with those who need their work.

How Image Donation Works

1

Photographers Donate

Creators upload their high-quality images to our platform, choosing which license to apply.

2

We Verify Quality

Our team reviews each submission to ensure it meets our technical and artistic standards.

3

Images Go Live & Community Grows

Approved photos are made available for free download to registered users. Photographers are credited, and the community shares how they've used the images.
